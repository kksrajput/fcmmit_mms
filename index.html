Meet Our Mentors

Club is led by a team who constantly reasons ,think & dares you to unlock your creativity around every pass. Having a network of good mentors is one of the most important thing in building coding skills.

If you are stuck at a problem and have invested a lot on that then you will boggle on your own skills and this will instate you directly on the wrong path .Having a mentor at that state will let your mind visualize clearly and you can perform better.

Everyone needs guidance and the mentors like Mr. Bhargava, Mr.Raghwendra, Mr. Gopinath and Mr. Pramoda have taken the initiative on (13-Sep-2019) and provided this Platform where all monsterminds join together and stroll towards path of learning.
