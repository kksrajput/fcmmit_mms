About us

Welcome to Monster Minds Club

Nagarjuna College of Engineering and Technology, Bangalore provides a platform for aspiring coders which is Monster Minds.
Monster Minds as a Coding Club encourages you in your venture to achieve your vision of being a authentic Coder.

For a Programmer , Challenges are inbuilt facets of their daily routine just like dinner and here we appreciate this habit by providing explicit contents to grab the opportunity.

Monster minds has the vision to acheive what is best for the coders .It inculcates the values of creating rather than copying. Monster minds exists to encourage, challenge and develop skills & anyone with an eye for programming are the sole benefiters. We offer a collection of code challenges that can help you prepare for upcoming job interviews with insigthul discussion accompanied by our team of enthusiastics mentors. Join Monster minds to transform from Ctrl+C & ctrl+V to Coding Savages.

Be Original rather than being a Replica.

01

Why Choose us

Joining monster minds will bring you closer to your vision of being an avid Coder . Regular coding competitions are organized to keep members engaged in the coding. Get Career guidance from experienced personnel. Test your knowledge and skills regularly by competing against the best.

02

Our Mission

Our mission is to support a diverse learning community where we help each other to attain fluency in programming.The frequent contests organised by the monster minds helps us in improving each day.

03

Our vision

We believe learning is more fun and efficient when we help each other along the way.We accomplish this by providing a space for students to build their own coding skills while developing: intellectual confidence, an inbuilt mindset with computational thinking skillset
