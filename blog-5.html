STEP TOWARDS ETHICAL HACKING

  • 25 Jun 2020
  • AJEET, 1NC18CS002 CSE,NCET
  • Informative

The mystery of hacking has attracted may people. It is a common belief that ethical hackers keep hacking websites or system, and by doing so you will be behind bars, but that is the only partially true (because that can land you in jail).

Ethical hackers are the only responsible for penetrating systems, but it is for a reason. They are supposed to provide solution to the problems relevant or related to cyber security. Cyber security agents are the people who understand the vulnerabilities of the system and then prepare the solution to prevent them from get hacked.

Getting Started

If you want to join or become an ethical hacker here are some steps or you can say them as key formulas you need know or follow so let’s start:

Step 1: TYPES OF HACKING

“Hacking” is a controversial word, and this for a reason and why it should not be? Because there are different types of hackers who hack for different purpose.
  • White hat hacker
  • Black hat hacker
  • Grey hat hacker

    • White and gray hat hackers usually use their hacking skill’s for good purpose for which world call them as “Ethical Hackers”, while black hat hackers use their skills for illegal purpose who effects the economy. Even though they serve different motives, organizations make use of all of them.

    The most ethical hackers are employed for the job which is titled as “Penetration Testing”, the penetration tester is a person who identifies the vulnerabilities in the application or website or system to protect it from external attacks. Who informs the organization of their faults in their system.


    Step 2: SKILLS & ACADEMICS

    There’re no fixed criteria to become a hacker. Every company has its own requirements but having a degree in Computer science, Information Technology or Mathematics will give you a great base of your ethical hacking career. Apart from this the main things that an ethical hacker needs to have are good problem-solving skills, ability to handle pressure and to think out of the box.


    Step 3: PROGRAMMING

    As an ethical hacker you are going to deal with the computer systems weather it may be 9-5 or whole night like an owl, thus a good command over programming languages such as C++, Java, PHP, Python, etc… Also, sharp knowledge about operating systems (OS) such as UNIX, Windows, LINUX and IOS.

    You can learn programming from various websites which offer programming courses such as Coursera, Udemy, Sampliner, and many more.


    Step 4: NO LIMITS FOR KNOWLEDGE

    It is saying that “a person never stops learning” like that your learning should not be limited by your educational background. There are many new and different hacking techniques coming up each and every morning so your knowledge should be updated.

    You can’t say that companies will hire you only because you have certification; you need to stand out and give solid reason for that why you are the most eligible Hacker in the town.


    Step 5: GET CERTIFIED

    When you are completely ready to burst your hacking skills on the monster called cyber criminals out over there, you need to get certified. The most trusted body that you can get certified with is the EC Council. Most companies would readily hire you, if you have this certification. To get certified, you would need to take a four-hour test which consists of 125 questions. the present version V10. You are required to take the certification test again, every time a new version is released.

    Almost all the MNCs employ full time ethical hackers, you can also find work in hacking consulting companies, military service as well as server provider companies. You can even have your own ethical hacking firm (Entrepreneur).
